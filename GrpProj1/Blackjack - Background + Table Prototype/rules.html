Rules of Blackjack



Goal of the Game:

  • Players should try to defeat the dealer by recieving a count that is as close to 21 as possible without going over.


Player Options:

Types of Moves
Description
Stand
When player chooses not the raise the value of their hand any longer and passes their turn to the next player.
Hit
When player wishes to accept another card from the dealer to achieve a hand value of 21 or a close but lesser value.
Double Down
During a player’s 2-card starting hand, he/she can choose to place a second bet equal to his/her first one to receive 1 card faced up and forfeit his/her turn.
Split
If the player’s first 2 cards’ rank matches, he/she can place another bet equaling his/her original bet to have the cards split into 2 hands. If either hand receives a card of matching rank, there’s an option to switch again. In addition, each hand is played one at a time.
